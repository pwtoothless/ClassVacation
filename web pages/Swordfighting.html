Sword Fighting

One of the many activities at the Wii Sports resort is Sword Fighting.

During this activity your goal is to knock your opponant off the high rise platform. Once you knock your opponant off you get a point.

This game is played best out of 3, so once you win two rounds you win the entire event.

During the daytime there are regular matches, and during the evening there are higher level matches.

Depending on your level you can also get different armor suits.
